About me

I am a Ph.D. student in the Computational Science, Engineering, and Mathematics (CSEM) program at the Oden Institute of UT Austin. I feel fortunate to have Prof. Per-Gunnar Martinsson and Prof. Rachel Ward as my advisors. My research interests lie in randomized numerical linear algebra and theoretical machine learning.
